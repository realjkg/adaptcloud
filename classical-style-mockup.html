I — Typography
Cinzel
Roman Inscriptional · Display
Used for all headings, navigation labels, subject names, and UI chrome. Derives from Roman stone-carved capitals — the letterforms of classical education and Catholic liturgical texts. Nothing like Inter or any modern sans-serif.

EB Garamond
Renaissance Scholarship · Body
Used for all body text, chat bubbles, and prose — including Bede's dialogue and the student's responses. Based on Claude Garamond's 1530 typeface; this is the font of Catholic printing houses, Greek lexicons, and great books curricula. Reads like ink on vellum.

Navy — Scholarly Authority
Primary interactive color. Buttons, sidebar active states, chat bubble (user), links. The blue of monastery scriptoria and academic regalia. Dignified, not corporate.
Gold — Sacred Emphasis
Faith integration cards, connect_to_faith tool responses, ornamental dividers. The gold of illuminated capitals and Byzantine icon halos. Never used for success/completion — that is emerald.
Verdigris — Natural World
New soft complement to navy + gold. Nature study subjects, outdoor-learning prompts, secondary borders. Oxidised copper — the color of bronze bells, garden gates, vellum edges. Not sage; richer, classical.
Parchment — The Page
Background for the entire app. Warm cream, not cold white. Signals "printed book" not "software screen." The same warm hue as aged laid paper.
Emerald — Discovery
Reserved exclusively for celebrate_discovery tool responses — the moment a student genuinely understands something. Not used for streaks, scores, or any gamification mechanism.
Claret — Rare Emphasis
New deep liturgical red. Use only for error states and cautionary notices. Never for success, never decorative. The color of a serious marginal note.

V — Design Philosophy
I
The Screen as Enabler
Cinzel is stone-carved, EB Garamond is ink on vellum. Neither looks like software. A parent who loves great books should feel they are opening a well-made volume, not launching an app. The screen should feel like a servant of learning, not its master.
II
No Dopamine Engineering
Emerald celebrate_discovery marks genuine understanding — it is never automatic, never scheduled, never tied to a streak. No progress bars that fill with confetti. No badges. The session ends at two hours, hard. It closes like a book.
III
Books Are the Point
The sidebar reads like a table of contents. Bede has no face, no mascot, no cartoon. He is a voice — the voice of a scholar who has read everything and asks the best questions. This is Charlotte Mason: Bede gives the Socratic prod; the great books give the content.
