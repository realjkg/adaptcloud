Bede Tutor
A Charlotte Mason AI tutor that lives on your home network — not in the cloud.
🏠 Runs on your LAN ✝️ Catholic curriculum 🎤 Voice-biometric child auth ✏️ Stylus + handwriting input 🔒 AES-256-GCM encrypted 📱 iPad PWA
Bede is an AI tutor inspired by the Charlotte Mason method — conversational, Socratic, and grounded in faith. Parents configure each child's daily plan on their own device; children connect from tablets and work through subjects guided by Sage, a gentle tutor persona backed by Claude. All data stays on your network. Your children's conversations are never sent to a third-party server.
What it does
🧠
Socratic Tutoring
Sage never just gives answers. Through carefully timed hints, celebrations, and reflective questions, children arrive at understanding themselves.
✝️
Faith-Woven Learning
Every session carries a configurable faith emphasis. Sage connects academic discoveries to Scripture, saints, and Catholic intellectual tradition.
📅
Daily Pod Planning
Parents build a "pod" — up to 10 students, each with their own subjects, grade, current unit, and faith focus — then hand out tablet links.
✏️
Handwriting Input
Children draw diagrams and write answers with any stylus — Apple Pencil, Livescribe, or inq.shop pens. Pressure-sensitive, no special drivers.
⏱️
Grade-Aware Timers
K–3 students get 20-minute subject blocks. Grades 4–8 use 60-minute blocks with automatic 10-minute break overlays — no parent intervention needed.
📋
Session Summaries
At session end, Bede generates a concise parent report: what was covered, what clicked, what needs follow-up — in plain, readable prose.
How a session works
1
Parent configures
Sets subjects, grade, current unit, and faith emphasis for each student via the web UI.
2
Child opens link
Navigates to the tablet URL, enters PIN, and optionally confirms identity with a voice passphrase.
3
Sage opens the subject
A subject-appropriate persona greets the child and begins a Socratic conversation — never lecturing, always asking.
4
Child responds
Text, voice (Whisper), or stylus drawing. Sage celebrates discoveries, offers hints, and weaves in faith connections.
5
Parent reviews
Session summary delivered after the last subject — a paragraph per subject with observations and suggestions.
"Education is an atmosphere, a discipline, a life." Bede is built around this principle: Sage never lectures, the room is always safe for wrong answers, and every subject connects to the living world — including the life of faith.

Technical overview
Component Technology
AI tutor Claude claude-sonnet-4-6 · streaming SSE · prompt caching · 400 token brevity cap · 4 agentic tools
Session summaries Claude Haiku · async, non-streaming · 600 tokens · delivered to parent at session end
Frontend Vite · React · TypeScript · Tailwind · Zustand · PWA (manifest + service worker) · iPad-optimised
Backend FastAPI · async SQLAlchemy · PostgreSQL · JWT (IP + UA fingerprinted) · rate limiting
Voice auth Resemblyzer speaker embeddings + MFCC similarity · Whisper transcription for enrollment phrases
Encryption AES-256-GCM · MASTER_SECRET → KEK → DATA_KEY hierarchy · all DB columns encrypted at rest
Deployment Docker Compose · Caddy (local CA, LAN TLS/443) → nginx → FastAPI · read-only containers, no shell
Handwriting HTML5 Canvas · Pointer Events API · pressure-sensitive · Apple Pencil / Livescribe / generic stylus

🔒 Privacy & Security

▸All data stays on your LAN — no student data reaches external servers
▸AES-256-GCM encryption on every database column at rest
▸JWTs are bound to device IP + User-Agent; replay from another device = 401
▸Voice biometrics verify child identity at session start — no passwords on tablets
▸ExfiltrationGuard middleware strips prompt-injection attempts from AI stream
▸Containers run read-only, no shell, all capabilities dropped
▸Constant-time credential comparison (hmac.compare_digest) throughout
▸Full encrypted audit log for every security event, independent of request transaction
Two perspectives

For Parents

Log in once on your device. Configure each child's subjects, current unit, and faith emphasis. Copy a link for their tablet. Review the session summary each evening — a paragraph per subject, observations, suggestions for tomorrow.

The parent dashboard shows the whole pod at a glance. Voice enrollment for new children takes two minutes.

For Children

Open the link on your tablet, speak your passphrase, and Sage greets you by name. Work through each subject at your own pace — type your answers, speak them, or draw them with your pencil. A timer shows how long is left; a break overlay tells you when to step away.

Sage never makes children feel wrong. Every answer is a starting point for a question.
